# Refund & Cancellation Policy

## 1. Order Cancellations

You may cancel any order **before** any of the following has occurred:

- Sample collection
- Test conducted
- Report issued

> **Note:** Once any of these steps has taken place, the sale is final and the order cannot be cancelled.

---

## 2. Complaints About a Test or Report

If you are unhappy with a test or the report you received, you may file a complaint. Complaints are reviewed according to the refund process described below.

### How to File a Complaint

- **Email:** support@bloodpanda.com
- **Phone:** Contact our customer support line

### Complaint Deadline

Complaints must be submitted **within 14 days** of the test date.

> Complaints received after this period will not be eligible for review.

---

## 3. Resolution Options

After reviewing your complaint, our support team may offer one of the following resolutions:

- **Full Refund** — Receive **100%** of the amount paid.
- **Free Re-test** — A new test will be arranged at the same or a different lab at **no additional cost**.
- **Wallet Credit** — The refund amount will be credited to your **BloodPanda Wallet** for future use.

---

## 4. When a Refund May Be Declined

Refund requests may be declined if our review determines that there was **no issue** with the test or the report.

---

## 5. Refund Payment Method

Approved refunds will be processed through one of the following methods:

- Original payment method
- Personal bank account (upon request)

---
